# Brew-Bean-Cafe-Sale-Tableau-Dashboard

## Cafe Sales Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

### Overview
This project analyzes café sales transactions to uncover revenue drivers, customer behavior, and operational insights.  
The dataset was cleaned and prepared using Python, then visualized in Tableau through an interactive dashboard.

---

### Business Objectives
- Track overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing cities and product categories
- Understand customer loyalty impact
- Analyze payment method preferences
- Monitor daily revenue trends

---

### Dataset
- **Rows:** 50 transactions  
- **Time Period:** January 2023  
- **Source:** Simulated café transaction data  

#### Key Fields
- Transaction ID
- Date & Time
- City
- Product Category
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Total Amount
- Payment Method
- Loyalty Membership

---

### Data Preparation (Python)
The dataset was cleaned using Python (pandas):
- Converted dates and numeric fields
- Standardized categorical values
- Removed duplicates
- Validated data types
- Verified missing values

Cleaned data was exported for Tableau analysis.

---

### KPIs Tracked
- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Transactions**
- **Average Order Value**
- **Top City by Revenue**

---

### Visualizations Included
- Revenue by City
- Revenue by Product Category
- Revenue by Payment Method
- Revenue by Loyalty Membership
- Daily Revenue Trend

---

### Key Insights
- Seattle generated the highest revenue among all cities
- Sandwiches were the top revenue-generating product category
- Card payments dominated total revenue
- Loyalty members generated higher total revenue overall
- Revenue peaked during mid-month periods

---

- **Python:** pandas, numpy
